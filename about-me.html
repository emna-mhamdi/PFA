I’m Maria Smith,
a mother & a food blogger

Hi there! I’m Sarra, the creator behind Yummy, where I share my love for cooking through simple, flavorful recipes designed for home cooks of all levels. My journey in the kitchen began as a kid watching my mom prepare family meals and it’s grown into a full-blown passion for creating dishes that are both delicious and approachable.

Whether you're looking for quick weeknight dinners, indulgent desserts, or healthy twists on classics, my goal is to make cooking fun and stress-free. When I’m not testing recipes or photographing food, you’ll find me sipping coffee while planning my next meal. Join me in making every bite count—let’s turn everyday cooking into something special!

Bringing you the tastiest recipes from kitchens around the world — whether you're a beginner or a seasoned chef, we’ve got something delicious for everyone.
